Welcome to Sparkalypse!

Sparkalypse is a project by Magic Of The Console (tm) about a certain disease that pushes those uninfected to the edge,
of living in pure hell never seen before.The infected people that got the name "Sparkled" now rules the land leaving
remaining survivors to live underground in dark bunkers that could only hold a limitedamount of people. Would we able
to conquer back the land and eradicate the disease? Only time can tell... Work your way through chats, dangerous
encounters, search through ruins, discover technologies and fight the fear of inhaling the ever terrifying "Pink Air"
thatmakes you sick and turn into a Sparkled along with the rest of the planet. Please do remember the gas mask whenever
you step out of the airtight bunker door!
