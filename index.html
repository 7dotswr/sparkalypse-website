Welcome to Sparkalypse!

The Sparkalypse is a project conceptualized by three users who go by the handles 7Dots, Harlottie, and Diamond!
They contributed varying amounts to pretty much everything here, so props to them!
Within the confines of the Sparkalypse, you will find a bunch of original lore, species, and worldbuilding.
Fair warning, its very concept is that an apocalyptic scenario fueled by outer-space parasites has occurred,
and that mutated furries exist in the resulting wasteland, so if you can't handle any of the suggested concepts, beware...
